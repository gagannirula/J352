Ingredients to Make Roasted Beets

  • 2 lbs. of beets
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • paprika

Recipe

  1. Warm up olive oil in saucepan
  2. Add beets
  3. Add salt and paprika
  4. Let cook until golden brown
  5. Slice and add a hint of lime juice
  6. Enjoy
